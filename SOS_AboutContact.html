About Us

Too Many Pencils Inc is the 743rd best seller of pencils nation-wide. We sell every kind of pencil under the sun and moon, all of which I have collected over my lifetime.

This company was founded after I had to literally rent out a warehouse to store the collection I had amassed. To this day I still have no idea why I ended up with so many pencils, but right now I just want them out of my life, and that takes precedence over rethinking my life choices.
